# Business Requirements Document

## Project Title
AI-Assisted Cybersecurity Risk Management Platform

## Problem Statement
The organization lacks centralized visibility into cybersecurity incidents, business risks, and remediation tracking.

## Objectives
- Track cybersecurity incidents
- Analyze business impact
- Prioritize organizational risks
- Improve executive reporting
- Support governance decision-making

## Stakeholders
- SOC Team
- IT Department
- Management
- Compliance Team
- Leadership
